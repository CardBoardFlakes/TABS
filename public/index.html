Welcome to TABSA (Totally Accurate Battle Simulator - Archive). Tabs is a very fun game with over 100 different wobblers at your disposal and more with the unit creator! But having so many units to manage can be troublesome and confusing. That is website is dedicated to help you manage your units. In the navigation bar there are 4 options Home, Manage, Archive and Music.

Features:
- Home
This is the homepage of the webapp, with links to all the different platforms you can purchase the game
- Manage
This page is dedicated to creating, archiving and deleting the units and factions you have created.
- Archive
This page serves two purposes, as an archive to see all the units stored in the database and assigning factions for units.
- Music
This page is dedicated to the soundtrack of TABS, where as you browse, manage units, assign them you can listen to your favourite tracks! This page can be accessed through the nav bar or by using the UI present on all pages in the bottom right corner.
