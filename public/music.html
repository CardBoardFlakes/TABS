Welcome to the music page, where you can enjoy some of the many soundtracks of TABS.
How to use the music tool:
In the playlist tab you can find a selection of different TABS soundtracks to listen to as you browse. You can filter between the different types of music, which are placement and battle. Furthermore you may also search up the names of the songs you wish to find.
Once you have chosen your desired song press play and enjoy!
Description of the music UI:
The music UI is at the bottom right of all pages in this webapp, the top displays the name of the track and the square brackets tell you if its a placement or battle theme. Simply click the pause button to stop or play the song and use the arrows to the left and right of the Play button to switch between tracks before or after the current track.
